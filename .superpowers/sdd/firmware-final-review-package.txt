## Approved deviation/root cause

The original 32 MHz BUSCLK / prescale 32 design is impossible on TIMG12 because it is the device's only 32-bit timer and has no prescaler; divider options are 1..8. The user approved fixed 4 MHz MFCLK / divider 4 / configured prescale 1 and period 4294.967296 s. This preserves TIMG12, exact 1 MHz, load 4294967295U, no interrupt, no autostart, and every existing physical mapping.
